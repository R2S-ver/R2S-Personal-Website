// DIY_Prototypes

PRJ_01 // TEXTILE_ENGINEERING

Modular Ballistic Carrier Study

This project was a deep dive into the **full technical sewing workflow**. Rather than just a defensive item, it served as a platform to master industrial textile design—spanning from initial patterning and material selection to the complex assembly of a modular load-bearing system (MOLLE).

Process Focus:
1. Design & Patterning: Translating ergonomic requirements into 2D flat patterns.
2. Material Sourcing: Investigating denier counts, abrasion resistance, and flame-retardant properties.
3. Construction: Larning heavy-duty stitching techniques for multi-layered composite textiles.
4. The MOLLE Logic: Understanding the PALS grid system for specific modularity.
