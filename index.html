// Prototypes

PRJ_01: Ballistic Armor System

An integrated multi-threat mitigation system focusing on civilian protection and structural modularity.

MATERIAL_LAYER_ANALYSIS

  • Outer Carrier: 1000D Nylon (Flame Retardant)
  • Hard Armor: 8mm Armor Steel Plate
  • Soft Armor: Multi-layer Kevlar (Slash/Stab Proof)
  • Spall Liner: 8-20 Layers Sateen Kevlar (4-8mm)
  • Trauma Pad: 10mm PU Foam (Energy Absorption)
  • Ventilation: 15mm Cooling Grid Pad

SYSTEM_FEATURES

- MOLLE Integration: Quick-attach surface for mission-critical gear.
- Modular Plates: Supports Ceramic (SiC), Steel, and Polyethylene inserts.
- Ergonomics: Dual quick-release buckles and adjustable shoulder panels.
